# Disciplefy — Marketing Site Promotion Playbook

**Version:** 1.1
**Date:** May 2026
**Context:** App available on Android, iOS, and Web. Marketing site at disciplefy.in, web app at app.disciplefy.in

---

## Strategic Positioning

**Lead with language identity and spiritual depth.** No technology buzzwords.

- **Hook:** "Understand the Bible Deeply — In Hindi, Malayalam & English"
- **Subheadline:** "Bible study guides in your mother tongue. Free to start."
- **Why:** Indian Christians respond strongly to language and community identity. The value is the output (deep study in your language), not the technology behind it.

**Current (too generic):** "AI-powered Bible Study for Everyone"
**Recommended:** "Bible Study in Your Language — Hindi, Malayalam, English"

---

## Target Audience Priority

### Primary: Malayali Christian Diaspora (Gulf + US + UK)

- Higher disposable income, strong willingness to pay for apps
- Tight community networks — word of mouth compounds fast
- Malayalam support is a genuine differentiator; almost no competitor does it
- Gulf diaspora has church WhatsApp groups (200-500 people) reachable through a single contact

### Secondary: Hindi-Speaking North Indian Believers

- Larger top-of-funnel but longer conversion path, lower ARPU
- Scale into this after proving the model with Malayalam audience

**Critical:** Do not treat these as one campaign. Different denominations, trust signals, content creators, and online behavior.

---

## Top 5 Channels (Ranked by Impact-per-Hour)

### 1. WhatsApp — Highest ROI, Zero Cost

**This week:**
- Identify 5-10 Malayali/Hindi Christians in your network active in church WhatsApp groups
- Give them free Premium access + ask for a 2-sentence personal recommendation with the marketing site URL
- Create 3 WhatsApp channels: Disciplefy Daily (EN), Malayalam, Hindi — even if starting with 10 people
- Daily verse broadcast at 6:30am IST with app link — repeatable daily activation loop

**Why it works:** A single recommendation in a 300-person Kerala church WhatsApp group generates 20-40 genuine installs that no ad campaign can match.

### 2. ASO (App Store / Play Store Optimization)

**App Store metadata (iOS):**

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Title (30 chars) | Disciplefy: Bible Study Guide |
| Subtitle (30 chars) | Study in Hindi & Malayalam |

**Play Store metadata (Android):**

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Title (30 chars) | Disciplefy: Bible Study Guide |
| Short description (80 chars) | Bible study guides in Hindi, Malayalam & English. Free to start. |

**Keywords to target (high-intent, low-competition):**

| Keyword | Competition | Why |
|---------|------------|-----|
| "malayalam bible study app" | Near-zero | No competitor targets this |
| "hindi bible study" | Low | Moderate volume, easy to rank |
| "bible study in malayalam" | Near-zero | Direct language intent match |
| "bible study generator" | Very low | Direct intent match |
| "disciplefy" | None | Own your brand term |
| "ബൈബിൾ പഠന ആപ്പ്" | Zero | Malayalam query, no competition |
| "हिंदी बाइबिल अध्ययन" | Very low | Hindi query, minimal competition |

**Do NOT target:** "bible app", "daily devotional" — YouVersion/Glorify own those terms.

**Screenshots (use all 10 slots on iOS, 8 on Play Store):**
- Frames 1-3: Language selector + study guides in EN/Hindi/Malayalam
- Frames 4-6: Core features (voice buddy, memory verses, daily verse)
- Frames 7-10: Social proof, trust elements

**Preview video:** 15-30 seconds showing study guide generation in Malayalam — the hero moment.

**Ratings strategy:** On Day 1, prompt your first 20-30 users (personal network) to leave reviews. Early reviews are weighted heavily by both App Store and Play Store algorithms.

### 3. Instagram Reels — Trust Signal Builder

**One Reel to make this week (30 min effort):**
1. Screen-record: ask the app "What does John 3:16 mean?" in Malayalam
2. Show the study guide appear in real time
3. Overlay text: "ഇത് Malayalam-ൽ ആണ്" (This is in Malayalam)
4. Caption: "Bible study that actually speaks your language"

This gets shared in church groups — that's the distribution mechanism. Don't rely on the algorithm alone.

### 4. Church/Pastor Seeding — Highest Quality Users

**Weeks 2-3:**
- Identify 10-15 pastors or cell group leaders on Instagram/Facebook who post Bible study content
- Personal DM (not template). Offer free Premium for 3 months, no strings attached
- **Kerala targets:** Marthoma, CSI, IPC youth fellowship leaders — they run structured Bible study programs

One pastor recommending the app to their congregation = 500 paid ad installs in retention quality.

### 5. SEO / Blog — Month 2+

**Quick win:** Per-book landing pages in Malayalam and Hindi
- "Romans 8 Bible Study in Malayalam" — near-zero competition
- "How to study the Bible in Hindi"
- "Bible study guide for John 3:16" (scalable per-verse pages)

**Your marketing site already has a blog.** Publish 2-3 genuinely useful articles before launch as trust anchors.

## Execution Timeline

### This Week (Pre-Launch)

- [ ] Set up 3 WhatsApp channels, get 50 subscribers via personal network
- [ ] Film 1 Reel showing Malayalam study guide generation
- [ ] DM 10 pastors with personal note + free Premium
- [ ] Finalize App Store + Play Store metadata (title, subtitle, keywords, screenshots)
- [ ] Post marketing site URL in 3-5 Indian Christian Facebook groups
- [ ] Verify mobile landing page has obvious download CTA above the fold (both iOS and Android links)
- [ ] Add "Get Early Access" email capture if not already present
- [ ] Audit marketing site: ensure no technology-focused messaging in headlines and hero sections

### Month 1-3 (Sustained)

- [ ] Daily verse WhatsApp broadcast — establish habit before asking for installs
- [ ] Church partnership with 2-3 Kerala churches (virtual demo, free access)
- [ ] Publish 1 blog post: "Bible study in Malayalam" (genuine, 800+ words)
- [ ] YouTube walkthrough in Malayalam (low production fine — authenticity > polish)
- [ ] Collect 5-10 user testimonials, add to marketing site within 30 days
- [ ] iOS App Store submission + optimize listing based on early Android data

### Month 3-6 (Scale What Works)

- [ ] Paid Meta ads — only after organic proof of what message converts
- [ ] YouTube Shorts repurposing Reels in Malayalam/Hindi
- [ ] Optimize both Play Store and App Store listings based on analytics
- [ ] Bible college/seminary outreach for institutional access

## Validation Questions (Answer Before Paid Spend)

1. **Day-7 retention rate** from current Android users? If <25%, fix retention before acquisition.
2. **Which language generates most study guides per user** — Hindi or Malayalam? Lead paid campaigns there.
3. **Free-to-paid conversion rate?** If <3%, the free experience needs work before marketing spend helps.
4. **Mobile landing page above-the-fold:** Is the download CTA obvious on phone? Most traffic comes from WhatsApp/Instagram on mobile.
