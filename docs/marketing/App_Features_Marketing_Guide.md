# Disciplefy App Features - Marketing Guide

**Last Updated:** December 2025  
**Version:** 1.0

## App Overview

**Disciplefy** is a Bible study companion app that helps Christians deepen their understanding of Scripture through AI-powered study guides, personalized learning paths, and interactive features. Available on Android, iOS, and Web.

**Tagline Options:**
- "Your Bible Study Companion"
- "Deeper Understanding, Faster"
- "AI-Powered Bible Study for Everyone"

---

## Core Features

### 1. AI-Powered Study Guide Generation

**What it does:** Users enter any Bible verse reference (e.g., "John 3:16" or "Romans 12:1-2") or a spiritual topic, and the app generates a comprehensive study guide in seconds.

**Study Guide Sections:**
- **Summary** - Clear explanation of the passage
- **Context** - Historical and biblical background
- **Interpretation** - Theological meaning and application
- **Related Verses** - Cross-references for deeper study
- **Discussion Questions** - For personal reflection or group study
- **Prayer Points** - Guided prayer suggestions

**Key Benefits:**
- Instant study guides (typically under 5 seconds)
- No more hours searching commentaries
- Perfect for sermon prep, devotions, or group study
- Theologically sound content based on orthodox Christian teaching

**Languages Supported:**
- English
- Hindi (हिन्दी)
- Malayalam (മലയാളം)

---

### 2. Daily Verse Feature

**What it does:** Fresh daily Bible verse with devotional content, delivered each day.

**Features:**
- AI-generated devotional content for each verse
- Available in multiple languages (English, Hindi, Malayalam)
- Share verse to WhatsApp, social media, or copy
- Streak tracking for consistent daily reading

**User Benefit:** Start each day with Scripture and spiritual insight.

---

### 3. Learning Paths (Structured Study)

**What it does:** Curated topic-based learning journeys following the  4-Step Bible Study Method.

**The 4 Steps:**
1. **Context** - Understanding the background
2. **Scholar** - Deep theological insights
3. **Group** - Discussion-ready content
4. **Application** - Practical life application

**Available Topic Categories:**
- Gospel Foundations
- Grace & Faith
- Discipleship
- Prayer Life
- Christian Living
- And more...

**Key Benefits:**
- Structured learning for spiritual growth
- Progress tracking across topics
- Complete at your own pace
- Based on proven Bible study methodology

---

### 4. Memory Verses System

**What it does:** Helps users memorize Scripture using spaced repetition learning.

**Features:**
- Add verses manually or from daily verse
- Smart review scheduling based on retention
- Flip-card review interface
- Progress statistics and tracking
- Quality rating system (Easy, Good, Hard, Again)

**User Benefit:** Actually remember the verses you want to memorize with scientifically-proven methods.

---

### 5. Voice Buddy (Voice Conversations)

**What it does:** Have spoken conversations about faith, Scripture, and spiritual questions.

**Features:**
- Speech-to-text input
- AI-powered responses about faith topics
- Text-to-speech playback
- Multi-language support

**User Benefit:** Like having a Bible study partner available anytime.

---

### 6. Listen to Study Guides (Text-to-Speech)

**What it does:** Have your study guides read aloud with natural-sounding voices.

**Features:**
- Section-by-section playback
- Adjustable speed (0.75x to 1.5x)
- Progress tracking within sections
- Skip between sections easily
- Works in English, Hindi, and Malayalam

**User Benefit:** Study while commuting, exercising, or doing chores.

---

### 7. Follow-Up Chat

**What it does:** Ask follow-up questions about any study guide you've generated.

**Features:**
- Contextual conversations about the study content
- Get deeper explanations
- Ask for clarification on difficult passages
- Conversation history preserved

**User Benefit:** Never feel stuck on a confusing passage again.

---

### 8. Save & Organize Guides

**What it does:** Save generated study guides for future reference.

**Features:**
- Save unlimited guides (with subscription)
- Access recent guides quickly
- Sync across devices when logged in
- Search through saved content

**User Benefit:** Build your personal Bible study library.

---

### 9. Personal Notes

**What it does:** Add your own notes to any study guide.

**Features:**
- Add personal reflections
- Notes saved with the guide
- Private and secure

**User Benefit:** Capture your insights and revelations.

## Target Audience Personas

### Pastor/Church Leader
- **Need:** Quick sermon prep, Bible study material
- **Feature Focus:** Study guide generation, learning paths, sharing
- **Pain Point Solved:** Hours spent searching commentaries

### Daily Devotion User
- **Need:** Consistent daily spiritual input
- **Feature Focus:** Daily verse, memory verses, streak tracking
- **Pain Point Solved:** Lack of structure in daily Bible reading

### Small Group Leader
- **Need:** Discussion material for group meetings
- **Feature Focus:** Discussion questions, sharing, learning paths
- **Pain Point Solved:** Finding relevant study material each week

### New Believer
- **Need:** Understanding Scripture basics
- **Feature Focus:** Simple explanations, learning paths, voice buddy
- **Pain Point Solved:** Intimidation by complex Bible passages

### Busy Professional
- **Need:** Study on-the-go
- **Feature Focus:** TTS, mobile access, quick guides
- **Pain Point Solved:** No time for traditional Bible study

## Messaging Guidelines

### Do Say:
- "AI-powered Bible study"
- "Instant study guides"
- "Deepen your faith"
- "Study anywhere, anytime"
- "Your personal Bible study companion"

### Don't Say:
- "Replaces your pastor/church"
- "The only Bible study tool you need"
- "AI that interprets Scripture for you"
- Avoid any claims about theological authority

### Tone:
- Warm and inviting
- Helpful, not preachy
- Practical and modern
- Respectful of diverse Christian traditions
