Cancellation and Refund Policy

Disciplefy: AI-Powered Bible Study Application

Effective Date: September 10, 2025
Last Updated: September 10, 2025
Policy Summary: Disciplefy operates on a token-based system where purchased tokens are generally non-refundable once successfully delivered. However, we provide full refunds for technical failures, payment errors, and other exceptional circumstances. All refund requests must be made within 7 days of purchase.

1. Overview

This Cancellation and Refund Policy governs token purchases made through Disciplefy, our AI-powered Bible study application. This policy applies to all token purchases processed through our payment gateway (Razorpay) and covers various scenarios where refunds may or may not be applicable.

Our token-based system allows users to purchase credits that enable the generation of AI-powered Bible study guides. Understanding when refunds are available helps ensure a fair and transparent experience for all users.

2. Token Purchase Model

2.1 How Token Purchases Work

  • Digital Delivery: Tokens are delivered instantly upon successful payment
  • Non-Physical Goods: Tokens are digital credits, not physical products
  • Immediate Availability: Purchased tokens are immediately available for use
  • Permanent Addition: Tokens never expire and are permanently added to your account

3. Refund Eligibility Criteria

3.1 Eligible for Full Refund

The following situations qualify for 100% refund:
  • Payment Processed, Tokens Not Delivered: Payment successful but tokens not added to account
  • Duplicate Charges: Multiple charges for single purchase due to technical error
  • Unauthorized Transactions: Fraudulent purchases made without user consent
  • Technical Service Failure: Inability to use tokens due to widespread system issues
  • Account Access Issues: Inability to access purchased tokens due to account problems
  • Payment Gateway Errors: Incorrect charging amounts or currency conversion errors

3.2 Eligible for Partial Refund

Partial refunds may be considered for:
  • Service Degradation: Significant reduction in service quality affecting token usage
  • Feature Unavailability: Key features unavailable for extended periods
  • Account Termination by Disciplefy: Account terminated due to policy violations (unused tokens only)
  • Extraordinary Circumstances: Natural disasters, emergencies, or other exceptional situations

3.3 NOT Eligible for Refund

No refunds are provided for:
  • Successfully Delivered Tokens: Tokens delivered and available for use
  • Used Tokens: Tokens consumed for successful study guide generation
  • Change of Mind: User decides they no longer want the tokens
  • Dissatisfaction with AI Content: Quality concerns about generated study guides
  • Account Deletion by User: User voluntarily deletes account with remaining tokens
  • Violation-Based Suspension: Account suspended due to terms of service violations
  • Alternative Service Preference: User prefers competitor services or manual study methods
  • Purchase Mistakes: Buying wrong token amounts or accidental purchases (unless within 1 hour)

4. Refund Scenarios and Procedures

Scenario 1: Payment Success, No Token Delivery

Situation: Payment processed successfully but tokens not added to account within 30 minutes.

Resolution:

  • Automatic system retry within 1 hour
  • Manual investigation if automatic retry fails
  • Full refund if tokens cannot be delivered within 24 hours
  • Alternative: Credit equivalent tokens to account

Scenario 2: Duplicate Charges

Situation: Multiple charges for single purchase due to technical error or network issues.

Resolution:

  • Immediate investigation of payment records
  • Full refund of duplicate charges within 3-5 business days
  • Retention of tokens from legitimate purchase

Scenario 3: Service Outage During Token Usage

Situation: System failure prevents token usage after purchase.

Resolution:

  • Service credits for affected users
  • Extension of daily limits during recovery period
  • Partial refund for extended outages (>24 hours)

Scenario 4: Fraudulent Transaction

Situation: Unauthorized purchase made using stolen payment information.

Resolution:

  • Immediate account investigation and security review
  • Full refund upon verification of fraud
  • Account security enhancement measures
  • Cooperation with law enforcement if required

5. Refund Request Process

5.1 How to Request a Refund

Step 1: Contact Support

Email support@disciplefy.in with your refund request within 7 days of purchase

Step 2: Provide Information

Include transaction ID, email address, purchase date, and reason for refund

Step 3: Verification

Our team verifies your purchase and eligibility for refund (24-48 hours)

Step 4: Decision

You receive email confirmation of refund approval or denial with explanation

Step 5: Processing

Approved refunds processed within 5-7 business days

5.2 Required Information for Refund Requests

  • Transaction Details: Purchase date, amount, transaction ID from email receipt
  • Account Information: Email address associated with Disciplefy account
  • Payment Method: Last 4 digits of card or payment method used
  • Detailed Reason: Specific explanation of why refund is being requested
  • Supporting Evidence: Screenshots, error messages, or other relevant documentation

7. Refund Methods

7.1 Primary Refund Method

  • Refunds processed to original payment method used for purchase
  • Credit card refunds appear as statement credits
  • UPI and net banking refunds credited to original account
  • Wallet payments refunded to original wallet balance

7.2 Alternative Refund Methods

If original payment method is unavailable:

  • Bank Transfer: Direct transfer to user's bank account (requires verification)
  • Account Credit: Equivalent token value credited to user account
  • Service Extension: Extended premium features or additional daily limits

8. Special Circumstances

8.1 Account Termination

  • Voluntary Account Deletion: No refunds for unused tokens
  • Policy Violation Termination: No refunds for used tokens; unused tokens may be refunded at discretion
  • Inactive Account Closure: Unused tokens refunded if requested within 30 days

8.2 Service Discontinuation

  • 30-day advance notice for service changes affecting token usage
  • Full refund of unused tokens if service discontinued
  • Migration assistance to alternative services if available

8.3 Force Majeure Events

  • Natural disasters, government actions, or other events beyond our control
  • Service credits or extended access provided during recovery
  • Refunds considered on case-by-case basis for extended outages

9. Dispute Resolution

9.1 Internal Review Process

  1. Initial Review: Customer support team reviews refund request
  2. Escalation: Complex cases escalated to management
  3. Final Decision: Management provides final determination on refund eligibility

9.2 External Dispute Options

  • Payment Provider: Dispute directly with credit card company or payment provider
  • Consumer Protection: Contact local consumer protection agencies
  • Legal Action: Pursue legal remedies as permitted by applicable law

10. Prevention and Best Practices

10.1 Avoiding Refund Situations

  • Start Small: Purchase small token amounts initially to test service
  • Check Connection: Ensure stable internet connection during purchases
  • Verify Payment: Confirm payment method details before completing purchase
  • Monitor Account: Check token balance after purchase to confirm delivery

10.2 Using Tokens Effectively

  • Understand token consumption rates for different languages
  • Use free daily tokens before consuming purchased tokens
  • Save frequently referenced study guides to avoid regeneration
  • Report service issues promptly to prevent token loss
