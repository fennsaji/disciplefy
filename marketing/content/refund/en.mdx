# Cancellation & Refund Policy

**Effective Date:** September 10, 2025 · **Last Updated:** September 10, 2025

> **Policy Summary:** Disciplefy operates on a token-based system where purchased tokens are generally non-refundable once successfully delivered. However, we provide full refunds for technical failures, payment errors, and other exceptional circumstances. All refund requests must be made within 7 days of purchase.

---

## 1. Overview

This policy governs token purchases made through Disciplefy, processed via Razorpay. Our token-based system allows users to purchase credits that enable the generation of AI-powered Bible study guides.

---

## 2. Token Purchase Model

- **Digital Delivery:** Tokens are delivered instantly upon successful payment
- **Non-Physical Goods:** Tokens are digital credits, not physical products
- **Immediate Availability:** Purchased tokens are immediately available for use
- **No Expiry:** Tokens never expire and are permanently added to your account

### Token Consumption

| Action | Token Cost | Refund Eligibility |
|---|---|---|
| English Study Guide | 10 tokens | Refundable if generation fails |
| Hindi Study Guide | 20 tokens | Refundable if generation fails |
| Malayalam Study Guide | 20 tokens | Refundable if generation fails |
| Failed Generation | 0 tokens | N/A — no token consumption |

---

## 3. Refund Eligibility

### ✅ Eligible for Full Refund

- **Payment Processed, Tokens Not Delivered:** Payment successful but tokens not added to account
- **Duplicate Charges:** Multiple charges for a single purchase due to technical error
- **Unauthorized Transactions:** Fraudulent purchases made without user consent
- **Technical Service Failure:** Inability to use tokens due to widespread system issues
- **Payment Gateway Errors:** Incorrect charging amounts or currency conversion errors

### ⚠️ Eligible for Partial Refund

- **Service Degradation:** Significant reduction in service quality affecting token usage
- **Feature Unavailability:** Key features unavailable for extended periods
- **Account Termination by Disciplefy:** Account terminated due to policy violations (unused tokens only)

### ❌ NOT Eligible for Refund

- Successfully delivered tokens (available for use)
- Tokens already consumed for study guide generation
- Change of mind or dissatisfaction with AI content quality
- Account deletion by the user with remaining tokens
- Account suspended due to Terms of Service violations
- Accidental purchases (unless within 1 hour of purchase)

---

## 4. Refund Scenarios

**Payment Success, No Token Delivery**
Payment processed but tokens not added within 30 minutes → automatic system retry within 1 hour → manual investigation if retry fails → full refund if tokens cannot be delivered within 24 hours.

**Duplicate Charges**
Immediate investigation → full refund of duplicate charges within 3–5 business days → tokens from legitimate purchase retained.

**Service Outage**
Service credits for affected users → partial refund for extended outages (>24 hours).

**Fraudulent Transaction**
Immediate account security review → full refund upon verification → cooperation with law enforcement if required.

---

## 5. How to Request a Refund

1. **Contact Support** — Email [support@disciplefy.in](mailto:support@disciplefy.in) within **7 days** of purchase
2. **Provide Information** — Include transaction ID, email address, purchase date, and reason for refund
3. **Verification** — Our team verifies your purchase and eligibility (24–48 hours)
4. **Decision** — Email confirmation of approval or denial with explanation
5. **Processing** — Approved refunds processed within 5–7 business days

**Required information:** Transaction ID · Account email · Payment method (last 4 digits) · Detailed reason · Supporting screenshots or error messages

## 7. Refund Methods

Refunds are processed to the original payment method:

- **Credit/Debit Cards:** Statement credits
- **UPI/Net Banking:** Credited to original account
- **Wallets:** Refunded to original wallet balance

If the original payment method is unavailable, we may offer bank transfer, account credit (tokens), or service extension.
