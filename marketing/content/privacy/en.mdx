# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** September 10, 2025 · **Last Updated:** September 10, 2025

> **Quick Summary:** Disciplefy is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect minimal personal information, use industry-standard security measures, and give you control over your data. You can use our app anonymously or create an account for enhanced features.

---

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Disciplefy ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our AI-powered Bible study application and related services (collectively, the "Service").

Disciplefy is designed to help Christians deepen their faith through AI-generated Bible study guides. We are committed to protecting your privacy while providing a meaningful spiritual experience.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information You Provide

- **Account Information:** Email address, name, and chosen authentication method (Google, Apple ID)
- **Profile Preferences:** Language preference (English, Hindi, Malayalam), theme settings, and app preferences
- **Study Content:** Bible verses, spiritual topics, and study guides you generate or save
- **Feedback:** Comments, ratings, and suggestions you provide about our service
- **Payment Information:** Transaction details through our secure payment processor (Razorpay) when purchasing tokens

### 2.2 Information Automatically Collected

- **Usage Data:** App interactions, features used, study guide generation frequency
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, app version, device identifiers
- **Technical Data:** IP address, browser type, session duration, error logs
- **Analytics Data:** Aggregated usage patterns and app performance metrics

### 2.3 Anonymous Usage

You can use Disciplefy without creating an account. Anonymous users receive temporary session identifiers and are subject to usage limits (3 study guides per hour).

## 3. Data Collection Summary

| Data Type | Purpose | User Control | Retention Period |
|---|---|---|---|
| Email & Authentication | Account management, sign-in | Account deletion | Account lifetime |
| Study Guides | Personalized experience, saved content | Delete individual guides | User-controlled |

---

## 4. How We Use Your Information

### 4.1 Primary Uses

- **Service Delivery:** Generate AI-powered Bible study guides tailored to your preferences
- **Account Management:** Maintain your account, preferences, and saved content
- **Personalization:** Customize the app experience based on your language and usage patterns
- **Communication:** Send important service updates and respond to your inquiries

### 4.2 AI and Content Generation

We use your input (Bible verses, spiritual topics) to generate study guides through third-party artificial intelligence services (OpenAI and Anthropic). Your input data is:

- Processed securely through encrypted connections to AI service providers
- Used solely for generating your study content and may be processed by our AI partners
- Subject to the privacy policies of our AI service providers ([OpenAI](https://openai.com/privacy), [Anthropic](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy))
- Not permanently stored by us but may be temporarily processed by AI providers
- Subject to content filtering to ensure appropriate, biblical content

### 4.3 Analytics and Improvement

- **Service Enhancement:** Analyze usage patterns to improve app functionality
- **Performance Monitoring:** Track app performance and identify technical issues
- **Security:** Monitor for suspicious activity and protect against abuse

---

## 5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

### 5.1 We DO NOT Sell Your Data

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

### 5.2 Service Providers

- **Supabase:** Database hosting and authentication — your account data and study guides are stored with Supabase
- **OpenAI/Anthropic:** AI services for study guide generation — your input prompts are sent to these providers for processing
- **Razorpay:** Payment processing for token purchases
- **Analytics Providers:** Aggregated usage statistics only (no personal identification)

### 5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights, ensure user safety, or investigate violations of our Terms of Service.

## 6. Data Security

### 6.1 Security Measures

- **Encryption:** TLS encryption for all data transmission
- **Database Security:** Row-level security and access controls
- **Authentication:** OAuth 2.0 implementation following security best practices
- **Access Control:** Employee access limited on need-to-know basis

> No system is completely secure. We continuously improve our security practices but cannot guarantee absolute protection against all potential threats.

### 6.2 Data Storage

Data is stored in secure, compliant cloud infrastructure with regular encrypted backups and geographic distribution.

## 7. Your Rights and Choices

### 7.1 Account Controls

- **Access:** View and download your personal data
- **Update:** Modify your profile information and preferences
- **Delete:** Remove specific study guides or your entire account
- **Export:** Download your data in a portable format

### 7.2 GDPR Rights (EU Users)

EU users have the right to: access, rectification, erasure, portability, object to processing, and restrict processing. Legal basis for processing includes contract performance, legitimate interest, consent, and legal obligation.

## 8. Children's Privacy

Disciplefy is designed for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (COPPA). EU users between 13–16 must obtain parental consent before creating an account (GDPR Article 8).
